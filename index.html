Pig Dice Game Instructions

Objective: Reach 100 points first!

How to Play:

  • On Your Turn: Roll the die repeatedly to add to your turn score.
  • Roll a 1: Lose all points for that turn and end your turn.
  • Hold Anytime: Add your turn score to your total and end your turn.
  • Win: The first to 100 points wins!
  • Tip: Balance risk and safety to win!
